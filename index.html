About Me

Urban Planner & GIS Specialist

I am an Urban Planner and GIS Specialist pursuing a Master's in World Heritage Studies in Germany, focusing on the integration of cultural heritage conservation into contemporary urban and regional planning. My expertise spans spatial analysis, remote sensing, and strategic planning, enabling me to deliver data-driven solutions that balance preservation with development needs.

My professional experience includes urban master plans, habitat assessments with disaster risk reduction strategies, eco-tourism frameworks, and urban design projects across diverse urban and rural contexts in Pakistan. I have worked with government agencies, NGOs, and local communities, ensuring that plans are technically sound, environmentally resilient, and socially inclusive.

I approach each project with a commitment to precision, sustainability, and cultural sensitivity, combining advanced geospatial tools with research based planning to create strategies that are both practical and future-ready.
